Rules

We use the normal version of the Hanabi game. There are 50 cards in total divided into 5 suits/colors. Each suit has 10 cards, each card has a number ranging from 1 to 5. The ten cards in each suit as distributed as follows amongts the ranks/numbers: three 1’s, two 2’s, two 3’s, two 4’s and one 5 card.

At the beginning of the game, the players collectively have 8 hint tokens and 3 mistake tokens. Each player is dealt 4 cards, these cards can only be seen by all the other players but not the owner itself. Now the players take turns one by one in a fixed order. For his/her turn, a player can choose to perform one of the following three action:

  2. Play:
